2008 Volkwagen Passat

The 2008 Volkswagen Passat will always hold a special place in my heart. Though it was a family car I got to drive it my senior year of highschool.

The car seats five but can easily hold 8 as my highschool cross country buddies can attest to. It doesn't claim great off road capabilities but functions surprisingly well off the pavement.

My biggest complaint about the car was the gas mileage. A sedan getting 17 miles per gallon (my personal mpg) is rough on a highschooler's wallet.

The 2008 Passat unfortunately met its demise when I rear ended someone at a yeild sign :(. Thought the damages weren't terrible it was an old car and thus was totaled by insurance.

2020 Volkswagen Jetta

The 2020 Volkswagen Jetta came about a couple months later. I was in the market for a new car and split the cost of this car with my parents. I now had skin in the game.

The Jetta is designed to be a A to B car much like a Honda Civic and performed fantastically for that purpose. The manual gearbox did offer some freedome, yet I felt very limited.

The 147 horsepower engine leaves some power to be desired, but I still pushed that thing pretty hard.

It did however sip gasoline and I regularly got 40-50 miles per gallon on the highway. I delivered pizza and doordash in the Jetta and it worked perfectly for that purpose.

I lost the Jetta in an accident on Red Road by campus, when I got distracted by a pedestrian and rear ended the person in front of me when he suddenly braked.
